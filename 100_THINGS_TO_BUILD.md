**How to use**

- In CrucibAI Workspace or “New project”, paste one of these as the prompt (e.g. “Build a todo app with auth and due dates”).
- Run the build, then refine in the editor and re-run tools (validate, security, export).
- Mix and match: e.g. “Todo app + Pomodoro timer” or “Dashboard from CSV + export to PDF.”
